Liverpool FC was founded in 1892 after a dispute caused Everton to leave Anfield.

The owner of Anfield, John Houlding, then created a new club, Liverpool FC, to play at the stadium.

Liverpool has won the European Cup/UEFA Champions League six times, more than any other club in England. Their victories came in 1977, 1978, 1981, 1984, 2005, and 2019.
